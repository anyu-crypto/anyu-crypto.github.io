## PhD Students

I am looking for self-motivated undergraduate students to work on cryptography and related areas.

Ideal candidates should have one of the following:

- **Solid mathematical background** — good training in algebra, number theory, probability, etc.
- **Strong programming skills** — ability to use programming to solve a wide range of practical research problems, such as deep optimization of cryptographic implementations and running cluster-based cryptographic attacks
- **Strong learning ability** and a passion for tackling challenging problems

## Postdoctoral Researchers

I am looking for cryptography PhDs, or mathematics PhDs with a strong interest in transitioning into cryptographic research.

- Standard package: 120,000 – 200,000 RMB per year
- Candidates with exceptional research background or strong cryptographic engineering skills may receive a more competitive offer
- Outstanding candidates may be recommended for the **Shuimu Scholar** ([水木学者](https://postdoctor.tsinghua.edu.cn)) program at Tsinghua University, which offers approximately 300,000 RMB per year

If you are interested, please feel free to email me.
